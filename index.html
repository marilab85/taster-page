Emergency Contact app

If you live alone you will find the information on how to ask for help

This is what you will need

  1. Gate code
  2. Emergency contact phone number
  3. Door code
  4. List of medicines you take
  5. List of hospitals you can go

Have all the information in the app and in case of emergency, you will hit the button and help will be on your way

Share the app link with neighbours as they will be the closest to you
